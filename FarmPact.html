How It Works

Step 1: Pre-booking for Price Security

Wholesalers can pre-book crops at an assured price, providing farmers with a safety net and ensuring a stable supply for wholesalers.

Step 2: Dynamic Pricing

If the crop is successfully harvested, farmers can benefit from a higher return, as wholesalers will pay more than the initial agreed price.

Step 3: Financial and Research Support

Wholesalers can contribute funds or bring in experts to help improve crop health, leading to better yields and healthier produce.

Step 4: Risk Reduction

Leverage crop health monitoring tools and weather data insights to reduce risks in crop production.

Step 5: Blockchain Security

All transactions and agreements are securely managed through blockchain, ensuring transparency and trust.

Payment and Crop Booking

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Farmpact?

Farmpact is a platform designed to create a secure, collaborative environment for farmers and wholesalers, ensuring sustainable agriculture and reducing risks.

How does pre-booking work?

Wholesalers can pre-book crops at an agreed price, providing financial stability to farmers and securing supply for wholesalers.

How is blockchain used in Farmpact?

Blockchain is used to manage all transactions and agreements securely, ensuring transparency and trust between all parties.

Who can join Farmpact?

Farmers, wholesalers, and researchers can join Farmpact to collaborate and contribute to sustainable agricultural practices.
